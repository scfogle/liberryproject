Welcome to the Enoch Pratt Library System!

Recent Additions

The Enoch Pratt Free Library is the public library of Baltimore City and the Maryland State Library Resource Center. It is one of the oldest free public library systems in the United States. For stories about experiences, events, programs, collections, and all kinds of treasures, check out the Pratt Chat blog.
